My Honeymoon

The Resort

The resort we stayed at was the Crown Paradise Club in Cancun. It is an all inclusive resort with many amenities. One of the best parts of the resort is the location. It is located in the hotel zone of Cancun and in our room we had an amazing view of the ocean. With it being an all-inclusive resort everything is included. There are many resturants to dine at ranging from Mexican, Italian, French, and other international cuisine.

Activity Description Fun Rating 1-10
Underground Rivers (Cenotes) In Mexico there are many underground rivers, known as cenotes, this one spanned a huge tunnel system underground. We were able to swim, explore, and ride little rafts through the rivers. 9
Ziplines In the same park, we were able to do ziplining. They weren't as fun as the other things. They didn't go very fast but they still gave a great view of all around. 6
Isla Mujeres Isla Mujeres was very cool. We traveled there on a boat and had a party going out. We then stopped on the island, went to the beach, and ate lunch. After lunch we drove golf carts all around the island. 10
